Rich Dad Poor Dad is a bestselling personal finance book by Robert T. Kiyosaki that challenges conventional beliefs about money and success. The book tells the story of two influential figures in the authorâ€™s life: his real father, the "Poor Dad," who valued job security, formal education, and saving; and his best friend's father, the "Rich Dad," who focused on financial literacy, investing, and making money work for you. Through these contrasting perspectives, Kiyosaki explores how wealthy people think differently about money, encouraging readers to shift their mindset, invest in assets, and take control of their financial future. The book offers practical lessons on budgeting, investing, and the importance of financial education, making it a guide for anyone looking to achieve financial independence.
